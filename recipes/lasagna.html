Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with various fillings. The dish is assembled in layers, then baked until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (9-12 sheets)
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Meat Sauce: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Bake: Cover the dish with foil (to prevent sticking) and bake for about 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  5. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna cool for about 10-15 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
